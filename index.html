About This Trip

This collection captures my 2024 trip to Tokyo — exploring the city's energy, peaceful temples in Asakusa, and unforgettable street food adventures.

My journey through Tokyo in 2024 was a dream come true — a blend of fast-paced city life, cultural depth, and mouthwatering cuisine.

From the electric energy of Shinjuku Crossing to the peaceful charm of Asakusa Temple, every corner of the city had something new to discover. I explored the vibrant anime and gaming culture in Akihabara

One of the highlights of my trip was the food. I had the iconic Ichiran Ramen in a cozy solo booth, enjoyed a fun and fast revolving sushi experience in Ueno, and marveled at the quirky variety of Tokyo's vending machines — from hot drinks to full-on meals!
